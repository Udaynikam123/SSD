About Us

Established in 2006 as a small tool room specializing in the manufacturing of jigs and fixtures, S S Designing and Automation has steadily evolved into a well-equipped facility with both conventional and non-conventional machining capabilities.

We are an ISO 9001:2015 certified company specializing in the manufacturing of precision machined components. We manufacture parts ranging from small batch quantities to high-volume production, ensuring consistent quality, accuracy, and timely delivery at every scale.

With a strong focus on engineering excellence and customer satisfaction, we are committed to delivering reliable and cost-effective solutions to diverse industries.
